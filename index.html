About S N Construction Company

S N Construction Company Pvt. Ltd. is one of the top construction companies operating in Noida, Delhi, Gurugram (Gurgaon), Lucknow, Ayodhya, and other major cities across Northern India.

Established in 2020, S N Construction Company is a leading provider of General Contracting, Design-Build, and Construction Management services. Our headquarters are based in Ayodhya. As the company continued to grow, so did our portfolio. Our team brings deep expertise across commercial, industrial, residential, educational, religious, and civil infrastructure projects.

To further our commitment to the region, we opened our second office in Noida in 2020. Over the years, we have developed strong relationships with architects, engineers, and talented subcontractors across North India. These collaborations have allowed us to help our clients in meaningful ways and become a true construction partner.

S N Construction Company was founded on the belief that integrity and quality workmanship are the cornerstones of good business. That same belief still drives us today. We actively encourage our team to engage with and support their communities — from local charities to civic initiatives, youth programs, and educational causes.

When you're facing a construction decision, you need professionals who will deliver your project on time, within budget, and to the highest standards. S N Construction Company is that trusted partner.
